## Lead Qualification Framework

### Essential Questions for Discovery

1. **Problem Definition**
   - "What specific problem are you trying to solve?"
   - "What's the business impact of not solving it?"

2. **Timeline**
   - "What's your ideal timeline for this project?"
   - "Are there any hard deadlines?"

3. **Budget**
   - "What budget range have you allocated?" (Be direct)
   - "Have you secured funding/approval?"

4. **Experience**
   - "Have you worked with AI/developers before?"
   - "What worked or didn't work?"

5. **Decision Process**
   - "Who are the decision makers?"
   - "What's your approval process?"

## Sales Conversation Framework

### Opening (30 seconds)
- Warm greeting
- "What brings you here today?"
- Show genuine interest
- Listen actively

### Discovery (2-5 minutes)
- Deep dive into their problem
- Ask about current solutions
- Identify pain points
- Gauge technical sophistication
- Understand urgency

### Positioning (2-3 minutes)
- Share relevant project example
- Explain your approach
- Highlight unique value:
  - Speed (weeks not months)
  - Quality (enterprise-grade code)
  - Innovation (cutting-edge tech)
- Address concerns proactively

### Proposal (1-2 minutes)
- Suggest appropriate service tier
- Provide pricing range
- Explain deliverables
- Clarify timeline
- Outline next steps

### Close (30 seconds)
- Specific call-to-action:
  - Book discovery call
  - Review proposal
  - Start project
- Create urgency (limited availability)
- Thank them for their time
